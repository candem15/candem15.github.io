Platform-Commands Microservices

We have two REST API that is called PlaformService and CommandsService. On our PlatformService we can do all CRUD operations about "Platform". On the other hand at CommandsService, we have "Commands" that related to "Platform" at PlatformService. These REST API's can be functional with their own even they are related to each other. For making this possible i used microservices architecture. In this project, it was kinda like my first steps to microservices world. It was very challenging and at the same time satisfying experience.
